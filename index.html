🚶 Domestic directions

By Train/Tube: We are ~7 minutes from London Victoria Station, a major hub for Underground, rail, and coaches. Exit Victoria toward Victoria Street/Vauxhall Bridge Road, turn left onto Vauxhall Bridge Road, and walk ~5–7 minutes. The house will be on your left.

By Coach: If arriving at Victoria Coach Station, it’s ~10 minutes on foot. Head east on Elizabeth Street toward Buckingham Palace Road, then go toward Victoria Station and onto Vauxhall Bridge Road.

By Car/Taxi: We do not have on-site parking. You may need a public parking garage (check Parkopedia). Taxis/rideshares can use the postcode to find us.

✈️ Airport directions

Book buses at nationalexpress.com, and trains at trainline.com. If traveling with 2+ people, a rideshare (Uber/Bolt) may be cheaper.

Gatwick (LGW):
Take a train directly to Victoria (~30 minutes, ~£15). You can tap in/out with contactless. Consider Southern Rail instead of Gatwick Express (only ~5 minutes slower, much cheaper). Note: from the South Terminal, leave extra time to reach the North Terminal (15 more minutes).

Stansted (STN):
Train to Tottenham Hale, then Victoria line to Victoria. You cannot use contactless from Stansted, so buy a ticket.

Luton (LTN):
Train from Luton Airport Parkway to London St. Pancras (~40 minutes), then Victoria line to Victoria (~15 minutes). Coaches also run direct to Victoria Coach Station (~1h15m).

Heathrow (LHR):
Piccadilly line (dark blue) from Heathrow, change at Hammersmith for District line (green) to Victoria (~50 minutes total).

🚇 Getting around

Public transport in London is excellent. Victoria Station is ~7 minutes away (Underground, trains, buses). The 24 bus near the house doubles as a scenic route through central London!

Use a contactless card to pay for Tube/bus (fares cap daily). London is walkable and has bike hires. You can also take a Thames river bus from Westminster (~£10, contactless accepted).
